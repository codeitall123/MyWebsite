Ethan Arrington My Story

My name is Ethan Arrington. I am an Eagle Scout, 4.0 BYU student and talented musician. I moved around the United States and Canada while growing up, and recently returned from a mission in Lithuania. I am preparing to apply to the Strategy Major in the Marriott School of Business next summer. I intend to earn a minor in Global Studies.

I currently consult social venture startups at the Social Venture Academy, and have been instrumental in one non-profit pitching for $10,000 in funding. I have been accepted to another consulting program, the Cougar Strategy Group, to consult for-profit companies next year. My father is an accomplished management consultant, and I aspire to also excel in that field.


Much of my experience comes from serving as a volunteer missionary in Eastern Europe. I was leading missionary efforts in the country of Lithuania when the Covid-19 pandemic struck. With my several fellow leaders, I quickly helped all the missionaries in the country pivot our efforts to online proselyting and teaching, and our numbers of people being reached and taught increased significantly during the pandemic. This demonstrated leadership ability and the ability to implement a strategic pivot in a time of crisis.


On that note, my favorite car brand is Ferrari (I'm a little obsessed, honestly), so I've decided to graciously provide you with their official website.
